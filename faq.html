Frequently Asked Questions

Game Related

How do I save my game progress?

Your game progress is automatically saved to your browser's local storage. To ensure your progress is not lost, avoid clearing your browser data.

What happens if I lose connection during gameplay?

The game will automatically pause if you lose connection. Once your connection is restored, you can continue from where you left off.

Technical Issues

The game is running slowly. What can I do?

Try these steps:

  • Close other browser tabs and applications
  • Clear your browser cache
  • Update your browser to the latest version
  • Check your internet connection speed

Account & Progress

Do I need to create an account to play?

No, you can play Monster Survivors without creating an account. However, creating an account allows you to save your progress across devices and participate in the global leaderboard.
