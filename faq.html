Frequently Asked Questions

Game Related

How do I save my game progress?

Your game progress is automatically saved to your browser's local storage. To ensure your progress is not lost, avoid clearing your browser data.

What happens if I lose connection during gameplay?

The game will automatically pause if you lose connection. Once your connection is restored, you can continue from where you left off.

Technical Issues

The game is running slowly. What can I do?

Try these steps:

  • Close other browser tabs and applications
  • Clear your browser cache
  • Update your browser to the latest version
  • Check your internet connection speed

Account & Progress

Do I need to create an account to play?

No, you can play Monster Survivors without creating an account. However, creating an account allows you to save your progress across devices and participate in the global leaderboard.

How can I track my high scores?

Your high scores are automatically saved locally. For cross-device synchronization and global rankings, consider creating a free account.

Can I play on multiple devices?

Yes! The game works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. Progress is saved locally on each device unless you create an account for cloud sync.

Gameplay Questions

What's the best strategy for beginners?

Focus on movement speed upgrades first, learn monster patterns, and don't rush into large groups. Check our Beginner's Guide for detailed tips.

How do weapon upgrades work?

Collect experience gems to level up and unlock upgrade choices. Each weapon type has unique upgrade paths that affect damage, range, and special abilities.

Is there a maximum level or end to the game?

The game features endless waves with increasing difficulty. While there's no technical maximum level, the challenge becomes extremely intense after 30+ minutes of survival.

Can I pause the game?

Yes, press the ESC key to pause the game and access the menu. This is useful for taking breaks during longer survival runs.

What are the different monster types?

The game features over 20 unique monster types, each with different behaviors:

  • Basic Zombies - Slow but numerous
  • Fast Runners - Quick but fragile
  • Giant Spiders - Medium speed with web attacks
  • Flying Demons - Aerial threats with dive attacks
  • Boss Monsters - Large, powerful, with special abilities

Browser Compatibility

Which browsers are supported?

Monster Survivors works on all modern browsers:

  • Chrome 80+ (Recommended)
  • Firefox 75+
  • Safari 13+
  • Edge 80+
  • Mobile browsers (iOS Safari, Android Chrome)

Do I need to install any plugins?

No plugins required! The game uses modern web technologies and runs directly in your browser without any additional downloads.

Why won't the game load on my device?

Common solutions:

  • Ensure JavaScript is enabled in your browser
  • Check that WebGL is supported (most modern devices do)
  • Try refreshing the page or clearing browser cache
  • Disable ad blockers if they're interfering with game assets

Updates & Features

How often is the game updated?

We release regular updates with new content, bug fixes, and balance improvements. Major updates typically arrive monthly, with smaller patches as needed.

Will there be multiplayer features?

Yes! Multiplayer co-op survival mode is planned for Q1 2025, allowing you to team up with friends for collaborative survival runs.

Can I suggest new features or report bugs?

Absolutely! We welcome feedback from our community. You can contact us through our contact page or email us directly at support@monstersurvivors.com

Performance & Optimization

How can I improve game performance?

For the best experience:

  • Close unnecessary browser tabs and applications
  • Use a wired internet connection for stability
  • Ensure your device has adequate RAM (4GB+ recommended)
  • Play in fullscreen mode for better performance
  • Lower your browser zoom level if experiencing lag

What internet speed do I need?

Minimum 5 Mbps for stable gameplay. The game primarily loads assets initially, so once loaded, it uses minimal bandwidth during play.
