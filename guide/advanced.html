Advanced Combat Guide

Character Skill Points

Swift (Movement Speed)

Highest priority, allows quick escape during intense boss waves.

Gun Slinger (Attack Speed)

Increases DPS output, particularly effective against boss groups.

Late Game Choices

Combine Longshot (Range) and Vitality (Health) based on boss types.

Team Composition

Standard Character Build

Sword + Swift/Gun Slinger/Longshot/Vitality combination

Echo Character Build

Recommended: Plasma Gun with Swift/Gun Slinger/Longshot/Vitality

Note: Ensure team has main DPS and tank, with support roles filling remaining slots.

Stage Progress & Pacing

  • Elite Stage Priority: High rewards with manageable risk
  • Pace Control: Strategic retreats, rest, and equipment upgrades
  • System Usage: Optimize energy and buff system timing

Tip: Don't focus solely on win rate; make good use of shop visits and chest openings.
