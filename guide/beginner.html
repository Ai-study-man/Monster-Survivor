Beginner's Guide

Basic Controls

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Auto-attack nearest enemy
  • Press Spacebar to use special abilities
  • ESC key to open menu

Early Game Progress

  • Prioritize movement speed and dodge abilities
  • Collect experience gems to level up
  • Maintain health by collecting supplies
  • Stay away from large enemy groups

First 10 Minutes Strategy

The opening moments are crucial for establishing a strong foundation. Here's your step-by-step survival plan:

Minutes 1-3: Initial Setup

  • Focus entirely on movement - avoid combat when possible
  • Collect any weapons you find, but don't rely on them yet
  • Learn the basic monster spawn patterns
  • Stay near the center of the map initially

Minutes 4-6: First Upgrades

  • Upgrade movement speed as your first priority
  • Take any damage upgrades that complement your starting weapon
  • Begin engaging smaller monster groups
  • Collect health pickups strategically

Minutes 7-10: Building Momentum

  • Start combining weapons for better damage output
  • Learn to kite larger groups of enemies
  • Upgrade your most effective weapons first
  • Prepare for the first mini-boss around minute 8

Common Beginner Mistakes

‚ùå Standing Still While Fighting

Movement is life in Monster Survivors. Even when shooting, keep moving in circles or figure-8 patterns to avoid damage.

‚ùå Upgrading Damage Too Early

Speed and survivability should come first. A dead character deals zero damage.

‚ùå Ignoring Weapon Synergies

Some weapons work better together. Experiment with combinations rather than maxing out single weapons.

‚ùå Not Learning Monster Patterns

Each enemy type has predictable movement and attack patterns. Study them to anticipate and avoid damage.

Equipment Choice

  • Sword and shield combo recommended for beginners
  • Ranged weapons require more skill
  • Focus on upgrading main weapon first
  • Use defensive gear to improve survival

Survival Tips

  • Keep moving, never stay still
  • Use terrain to avoid enemies
  • Watch for item indicators on minimap
  • Retreat from strong enemies when needed
